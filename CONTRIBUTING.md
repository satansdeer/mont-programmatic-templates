# Contributing

Thanks for contributing to Mont programmatic templates.

By submitting a contribution, you confirm that:

- You wrote the contribution or have the right to submit it under this repository's license and contributor terms.
- Your contribution does not include third-party logos, product names, screenshots, icons, fonts, UI assets, trade dress, or other protected material unless explicitly disclosed and approved.
- Your contribution may be used, modified, redistributed, sublicensed, and commercially used in Mont products and services.
- Generated video outputs created with templates from this repository may be used commercially without attribution solely because the template was used.

## Template Requirements

Every template must include:

- `template.tsx`
- `manifest.json`
- `README.md`
- A preview asset, usually `thumbnail.png` and optionally `preview.mp4`

Templates should be generic and brand-safe. Prefer names like `mobile-chat-thread`, `team-chat-board`, or `app-notification-list` instead of names tied to third-party products.

## Review Policy

Maintainers may reject or remove templates that:

- Copy or closely imitate a third-party product UI.
- Use third-party names, logos, marks, icons, screenshots, or proprietary assets.
- Create confusion about affiliation, sponsorship, or endorsement.
- Include undisclosed third-party assets.
- Are too brittle, visually broken, inaccessible, or hard for agents to configure.

See `docs/legal-template-policy.md`.
