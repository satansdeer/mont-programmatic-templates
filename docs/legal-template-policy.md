# Legal Template Policy

This repository accepts generic, original, brand-safe templates.

## Do Not Submit

- Product-specific UI clones such as "iMessage", "Slack", "Notion", "Linear", "Figma", or other named product interfaces.
- Third-party logos, icons, trademarks, product names, screenshots, fonts, or copied visual assets.
- Templates that imply endorsement, sponsorship, or affiliation.
- Templates based on confidential customer material.
- Templates using third-party assets without clear license disclosure.

## Prefer Generic Equivalents

Allowed examples:

- `mobile-chat-thread`
- `team-chat-board`
- `kanban-status-board`
- `calendar-event-card`
- `notification-feed`
- `product-demo-window`

Avoid exact product names and exact product visual identity. A generic blue/gray mobile chat interface can be acceptable; an Apple Messages clone named "iMessage" is not.

## Generated Outputs

Rendered videos and images created by users with templates from this repository may be used commercially without attribution solely because a template was used. Users remain responsible for the content they add to the output.

## Review

Maintainers may reject, edit, unpublish, or remove templates for legal, safety, quality, or brand-risk reasons.
